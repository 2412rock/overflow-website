Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29 September 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our game, including when you sign in with an email and password or Google Sign-In. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users:

  • Personal Information: We collect your email address when you sign up using either email and password or Google Sign-In. The email is stored in our system to facilitate login, communication, and account management.
  • Authentication Information: If you sign up using email and password, we collect and store your password securely using industry-standard encryption methods. If you sign in using Google, we receive and store your email address associated with your Google account.
  • Automatically Collected Information: We may collect data on how you interact with the game, including game progress, achievements, and gameplay statistics. This information is used to improve the game and provide you with a better experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create, manage, and authenticate your account, ensuring a secure and personalized gaming experience.
  • Game Features: To provide game functionalities, such as saving progress, unlocking features, and displaying achievements.
  • Communication: To send important notifications related to your account, such as security alerts, game updates, and administrative messages.
  • Analytics and Improvements: To analyze usage data for improving game performance, troubleshooting bugs, and enhancing the overall user experience.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to ensure the safety of your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of passwords and sensitive data.
  • Limiting access to your personal data to authorized personnel only.
  • Using secure servers and encryption protocols to protect data during transmission.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the game, such as cloud storage or analytics services. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your information for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or safeguard the security of our users.

Google Sign-In

If you use Google Sign-In, please note that your use of the Google services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We only collect and store the email associated with your Google account and do not have access to other personal data or information from your Google profile unless explicitly authorized by you.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with game services. If you delete your account, we will securely delete or anonymize your data, except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request to delete your personal information by contacting us at the email provided below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
